Comments load when your Meta app has the instagram_manage_comments permission and you reconnect Instagram. You can delete individual comments from the dashboard when comments are loaded. Post delete uses Meta’s API: it can remove many of your professional account’s posts, but not “anything” (for example some ads/boosted items or disallowed placements). Carousel slides are handled by deleting the whole carousel album.
